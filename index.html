It starts with a mindset

Tackle problems with curiosity and enthusiasm. Everyone will have ideas, which you should keep handy, but first just listen and learn. Get to know the business, products, service, market, and competitors. Most importantly, the users or customers - discover who they are and the current experience before you decide how to change it.


Spend time in the problem space, establish shared understanding, and build empathy for the user. When you discover the problems that are worth solving, promote different perspectives, we're multi-disciplinary teams for a reason. Unite and motivate teams by leveraging your user insights and data, while facilitating communication and collaboration. Start making educated guesses that are measurable.
